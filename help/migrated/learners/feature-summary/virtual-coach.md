# Introduction

The Virtual Coach is a transformative feature within Adobe Learning Manager designed to bridge the critical gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. Rather than relying solely on passive learning methods, videos, quizzes, and reading materials. Virtual Coach provides learners with an interactive, immersive environment to practice high-stakes conversations and learn soft skills.  

Think of Virtual Coach as a "24/7 practice partner" and "flight simulator for communication." Just as pilots log thousands of hours in flight simulators before handling real aircraft, employees can practice complex conversations with an AI-driven persona before they encounter similar situations with real clients, customers, or team members. The Virtual Coach adapts in real-time to learner inputs, providing instant, data-driven feedback on both what they said (knowledge) and how they said it (delivery and style).
